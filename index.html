Explore Vilnius!

See what this city has to offer!

What is there to explore?

Explore our city's history

Vilnius has a been established 700 years ago. It has a long history which is still around to be seen to this very day.

Explore our nature!

Our city is one of the greenest cities across europe. Around 61% of Vilnius is covered in greenery that is around majority of the year. Ofcourse as long as there is no snow.

Discover our cuisine!

Discover our traditional food, among many restaurants and cafes!

Discover art!

Like many other cities Vilnius has plenty of art of its own. Street arts for those who enjoy vibrant looking city and modern art galeries for those who prefer it.

Meet new people!

Meet new people and make new friends, Lithuanian people are very friendly, by getting to know them you might find new and interesting places to visit and enjoy your time more. It's always better to explore with friends.

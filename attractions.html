Current and upcoming celebrations

Uzgavenes

Uzgavenes is a deeply rooted tradition for chasing out winter from our world. It is celebrated towards end of winter. This tradition is similar to Halloween where people make up their own costumes and masks in order to scare away winter. It includes dressing up burning of "More" its an effigy that represents winter it self. It usually includes folkore music, dancing and a staged fight between "Lasininis" and "Kanapinis" who represent change of diet and look of people chaning from winter into spring.
